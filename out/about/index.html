Overview

Approximately 5000 people attended in 2012. The event was a roaring success, with many people from neighbouring areas joining locals for a joyous day of fun and festivities. The festival once again proved what a great place Crystal Palace is to live and visit, and after community consultation the organisers are looking forward to a festival that fulfils the priorities for the area in 2013.

FAQs

Q. How is the festival staffed and organised?
A. The festival is being run entirely voluntarily – the organisers are all donating their time and skills for free. Local musicians, artists, performers and arts workshop leaders are also donating their time and talents to make this a great festival for their community.

Q. How is the event funded?
A. Local businesses sponsored the festival and bought advertising in the What’s On Guide and website. Food and crafts stalls also brought in income and various fundraising activities took place, including a raffle on the day of the festival.

Q. How were these funds used?
A. An extensive campaign of posters, flyers and What’s On Guides were funded to draw the audience of 5,000 people to the festival. A live music stage and PA, stalls and tents were hired. Funds also paid for insurance, toilets, first aid and park hire.

Q. How can I get involved?
A. Please email volunteer@crystalpalacefestival.org or read our volunteering page.

Q. How can I trade at the festival?
A. We are afraid that the stalls have now sold out. Please check back early in 2014 for opportunities at next year’s festival.
