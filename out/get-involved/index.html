Get involved

To make this year's Crystal Palace Overground Festival bigger and better than ever before we need you to get involved! Local businesses and traders, community groups, artists and churches are what make the festival the fantastic event it is, so find out how you can join in this year.

Sponsors

We’ve got very good at doing a lot with very little, but sponsorship from local businesses is invaluable. Sponsorship packages start from as little as £100 and enable us to keep the festival going in the face of massive arts cuts by local authorities.
Find out more

Local businesses

If you’re a local business or trader holding a festival event, advertising in the What’s On programme guide is a great way to get exposure. The guide is distributed throughout the local area and also featured on the festival website: excellent value for money.
Find out more

Community groups

Being part of the festival is a great way to engage with the Crystal Palace community, so if you’re a local community group, school, church or artist with a festival event you can be promoted in our What’s On programme guide. It’s the perfect way to get more involved.
Find out more

Advertisers

Our What’s On programme guide has a distribution of 8,000 throughout the local area, both before the festival and over the festival weekend. With advertising rates starting from just *£45 the guide provides great exposure for local businesses.
