What’s coming up…

We’re busy lining up a bumper weekend of festival fun, including performing arts, crafts, holistic and sports activities, live music, DJs and food, plus antique, craft and vintage shopping throughout the Crystal Palace Triangle. Check out last year’s programme to get an idea of what’s in store

Save the date! Saturday, 10th May. Another chance to get involved in the 2014 festival. From 12 midday the festival team will be outside Sainsbury’s, Crystal Palace spreading the festival love. Find out more about volunteering and what's happening during the 2014 festival.

Sponsorship

The best things in life (like the Crystal Palace Overground Festival) are free! But there are still costs to put on an event like this. Become a sponsor and get not only great exposure, but a nice warm feeling too.

Volunteer

Last year’s festival was a huge success thanks to the fantastic teams of volunteers. If you can spare a few hours leading up to or during the festival, take a look at our Volunteers page for more info on how you can be part of the festival.
