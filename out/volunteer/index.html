Volunteer

Help make the 8th Crystal Palace Overground Festival bigger and better!

Are you passionate about festivals, having fun and want to get more involved in your local community? If so apply today or read below to learn more.

Join the really useful team today!

We need all kinds of like-minded people to volunteer and help make the 8th Crystal Palace Overground Festival bigger and better than ever before.

Jobs include stage stewards, programme distributors, experienced crowd controllers and donation collectors.

Our volunteers usually work a 4-hour shift across any of the 4 days of the festival, from a fringe event around the Crystal Palace Triangle to Westow Park itself. If you have time or a skill we can use it!

Help make the 2014 festival the best one yet!

Applicants must be aged 18+ and be able to commit to working a 4-hour shift. In return we offer training, a certificate for your participation, good times and an opportunity to meet new friends, whilst making Crystal Palace an even better place to live!

Join the really useful team today or email volunteer@crystalpalacefestival.org.
