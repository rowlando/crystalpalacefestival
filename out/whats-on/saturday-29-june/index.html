Saturday 29 June

Saturday features the big park event in Westow Park with more festival shenanigans than ever before! Our kids' zone is situated up by the Church Road entrance to the park and has everything from the circus to hulahooping to storytelling. The performing arts tent is one that you just don't want to miss with an extra special production from LAStheatre featuring our very own local artistes. You can't miss the main music stage, but you wouldn't want to anyway, with music from south London acts to blow you away. You will be relaxed and soothed by our holistic tent and stallholders into a world of bliss and fed and watered by our food tasting tent. Not to mention exercise for those who fancy getting fit, community stalls to find out what’s going on in Crystal Palace, art and craft stalls and tent for those who fancy a purchase. There's tons more to discover in the park so check it all out here!

But it's not just about Westow Park on Saturday – there's also loads going on in our venues, shops and markets in the area known locally the Triangle – Westow St, Church Road, Westow Hill and Central Hill. The Transition Town peeps are holding their weekly food market in Haynes Lane, all of our lovely pubs have great entertainment, food and drink, the Phoenix Centre hold their annual fayre – just some of the festival events that you will love!
