Westow Park: Performing Arts

Sponsored by

Step inside and enter a Victorian wonderland of curiosities and oddities with theatrical, inventive and mind-boggling interpretations of The Great Exhibition and beyond! Let your hair down, loosen your corset and twiddle that moustache whilst poets, bands and theatremakers take to the main stage.

Or perhaps take an afternoon perambulation around our Cabinet of Curiosities to meet inspiring minds and take part in workshops that inform and amaze. With a mixture of local talent and theatrical beaux from further afield the LAStheatre team and festival producers create an event which celebrates the inquiring mind of the Victorian and the golden age of the Crystal Palace!

LAStheatre specialises in creating interactive and immersive theatrical events. We create worlds with our audience – transforming spaces through play and adventure, spirit and imagination. We strive to incorporate language, form and narrative to create performances that blur reality. Ultimately, we aim to leave a legacy for our audience: the newfound confidence to make-believe, anywhere – www.lastheatre.com

Facilities

Facilities in the park include food court, three festival bars, festival HQ and meeting point, St John’s Ambulance, toilets (kids’ zone and lower end of park), community information and festival stewards to keep you safe.
