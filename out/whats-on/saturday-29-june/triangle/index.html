The Triangle: What's going on?

We who live in Crystal Palace have a lovely shopping, grazing and drinking area we call the Triangle. If you are a visitor to the area for the festival, don’t miss out on the many events that are happening all around streets of the Triangle and beyond down Gipsy Hill at the very lively Gipsy Hill Tavern!
