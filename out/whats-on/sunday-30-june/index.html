Sunday 30 June

Now Saturday’s a big day, but you won’t want to stay indoors on Sunday 30 June as the festival fun continues all around the Triangle, and at other venues such as Crystal Palace station and the Gipsy Hill Tavern.

Kicking off with Soundtracks our very own classical and resonant music concert at Crystal Palace station, wending its way throughout the Triangle and down Gipsy Hill with events at traders and restaurants such as Papa Gaio, Mediterrenea, The Bookseller Crow, Acorn our lead sponsors and Bambinos, the festival fun comes to our shops and stations. If you fancy being sporty, and you are not too hungover from Saturday, check out the fitness fun event at Victory Place (both Saturday and Sunday).

New for 2013 organisers have been busy creating some events to bring the festival to the streets of Crystal Palace:

  • Kaleidoscope – a new festival initiative in partnership with Haynes Lane’s traders, a fine art and craft market from 12pm – 6pm
  • Vintage fun on Church Road - celebrating Crystal Palace’s vintage and antiques culture, come along in your best vintage gear to check out our new hub – open from 12pm til 6pm!
