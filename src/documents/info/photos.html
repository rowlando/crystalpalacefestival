Thank you to everyone who has taken part in the festival - punters, volunteers, sponsors, traders, musicians, dancers, artists, actors, performers, foodies, the local community, Westow Park, pubs, the weather, shopkeepers and their staff, festival committee, operations peeps… the list goes on

Your photos

We're really keen to see your pics of the festival. We have set up a Flickr group where you can share your memories of festival. You'll need to join the group before you can add pics to the group (and Flickr, if you're not a member already). There's lots going on over the 4 days and there's no way we can capture everything, so please contribute your favourite snaps via Flickr – Thank you!
