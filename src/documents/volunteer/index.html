We hope you can volunteer for the Crystal Palace Overground Festival – just fill out the form below. This is a great opportunity to get involved and meet new people in your friendly local area. We are looking for people to work together to enable a fun and safe festival that includes and entertains all of our community.

When do we need your help?

  • Thursday & Friday: Fringe Events
  • Saturday: Westow Park / The Triangle
  • Sunday: The Triangle / Venues around Crystal Palace

We'll employ a shift pattern so you can enjoy the festival too!

What help do we need?

  • Stewarding
  • Programme distribution
  • Providing information
  • Visitor support in the Triangle area
  • Fringe events
