Thursday 27 June Read more about Thursday

Lots of kids' stuff today with events at Upper Norwood Library, Crystal Palace FC at Westow Park and table tennis at the Methodist Church Lower Hall. For the adults there's Irish folk music at the Gipsy Hill Tavern, a six course beer and food matching event at Westow House and comedy at Brown & Green at Crystal Palace station – see the full programme here.

Friday 28 June Read more about Friday

A cultural feast for film and spoken word enthusiasts today with a special Spoken Word night at Antenna Café Thing and open air cinema at Coopers Yard. The cinema features a rare showing of The Pleasure Garden shot in Crystal Palace park in 1953.

Other events include a reminiscence morning at the library, bingo and a tea dance moving on into the evening with lots of retro entertainment. Westow House's 1950s extravaganza will transport you back to the rock ‘n' roll era while you can dance the night away to some of the best classic songs at The White Hart. For something different check out the Guinness and oyster fest at the Gipsy Hill Tavern – see the full programme here.

Saturday 29 June Read more about Saturday

There's so much going on – you can find the full programme here. Highlights include Westow Park where you will find the best in food and drink, performing arts, kids' entertainment, holistic, exercise and green activities not to mention the best music in south London this summer on the main stage. There's tons going on in the Triangle – with community fairs and markets in Haynes Lane, Phoenix Centre and Methodist Church, fitness fun at Victory Place and lots lots more. After the park head off to our great pubs for more festival live music or a dedicated vinyl night if that's your bag at Antenna Café Thing.

Sunday 30 June Read more about Sunday

Save some energy for Sunday as the festival gang have worked hard to bring you a whole new day of events along with our fab local traders and pubs. New for 2013 is our hub celebrating local vintage culture on Church Road and also Kaleidoscope our fine art and craft market in Haynes Lane. Come outside in the Triangle and down the hills for great events – from classical music in Crystal Palace station, blues at the Gipsy Hill Tavern, more live music outside at Westow House to jazz at the Grape & Grain – find out more about venues and other events on Sunday. We think you'll find the only way to round off the weekend is to take the Monday off!
